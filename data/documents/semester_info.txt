ACADEMIC CALENDAR AND IMPORTANT DATES

Semester End Date
The semester ends on April 30, 2026.

Academic Calendar
The academic year consists of four semesters.
Semester 1 runs from February to April.
Semester 2 runs from May to July.
Semester 3 runs from August to October.
Semester 4 runs from November to January.

Graduation Ceremony
The 2026 graduation ceremony will be held on June 15 at the main auditorium.

Academic Year Start
The academic year starts in February 2026.

HDC Meetings
HDC meetings occur monthly on the first Tuesday of each month.
In 2024, there were 12 HDC meetings.
